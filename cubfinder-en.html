Cubfinder

Cubfinder is a comprehensive platform for optimized cubicle management and reservation. The system maximizes user flow efficiency through real-time monitoring and a robust security scheme integrated with Azure. In my dual role, I ensured software stability as QA through rigorous testing, and led Front-End development in Android Studio, transforming high-fidelity Figma prototypes into an intuitive and functional interface.
